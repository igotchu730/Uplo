Uplo's Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Uplo! We are a file-sharing website for sharing and enjoying videos/photos/files with others. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information about you, our users, when you use our website.

Description of Service

Uplo allows users to upload files and generate temporary links for sharing. Files are stored on AWS S3 and are automatically deleted after a limited time.

Information We Collect

  • Device & Usage Data: When you upload on our website, we collect your IP address, browser type, cookies, time stamp, location data.

  • User-Generated Content: Uplo has non-exclusive access to all user uploaded content. For more information, refer to our User Content as listed under Terms of Service.

Content You Upload

We collect and store the videos and photos you upload, including any metadata associated with the content, such as location tags or camera details.

Communication Data

If you contact us directly, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and the content of your messages.

Usage Data

We collect information on how you interact with our website, which can include visited pages, viewed content, clicked links, and other actions.

We do not sell your data. However, we may share it if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

Children's Privacy

Uplo is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), if we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

If Uplo were to collect personal information from children under 13, we would first obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting, using, or sharing such information. Parents who believe their child has provided us with personal information without consent may contact us at uplo.support@myspace.com to request its removal.

The information or user data we obtain is as follows:

  • To enforce limits and prevent abuse.

  • To improve and personalize your experience on our site.

  • To understand how users interact with our services and to improve our offerings.

  • For the purpose of operating, developing, providing, and using the website.

  • For security purposes, to prevent fraud, to comply with legal obligations, and to ensure the integrity of our site.

Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

  • Right to Opt-Out of Data Sharing: Opt out of the sharing of your personal data with third parties for targeted advertising.
