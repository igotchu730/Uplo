About Us

At Uplo, we believe in seamless and efficient file sharing. Our platform enables users to upload files and generate shareable links, eliminating the common frustration of file size restrictions imposed by many messaging and email services.

With Uplo, sharing large files becomes effortless. Simply upload your file, receive a unique link, and share it with your peers—no accounts, no complicated steps, just instant accessibility. Each shared link directs users to the uploaded file, which remains available for 24 hours before being automatically removed, ensuring security, privacy, and optimal storage management. Users can upload up to three files per day, with a maximum file size of 2GB per upload, maintaining efficiency and fair usage for everyone.

We are committed to providing a fast, reliable, and user-friendly file-sharing experience, empowering users to collaborate and communicate without barriers.
